About GTR EDUTECH

Transforming Education Across AP & Telangana

Founded with a mission to democratize quality education, GTR EDUTECH has been at the forefront of competitive exam coaching for institutional partners across Andhra Pradesh and Telangana. We believe every student deserves access to world-class guidance, regardless of their institution's location or resources.

Our zero-investment partnership model eliminates financial barriers for schools and junior colleges. We deploy expert faculty directly to your campus, provide all study materials, and manage the entire coaching operation—allowing your institution to offer premium competitive exam preparation to your students without any burden on your budget.

With a track record of training 25 students with 14 NIT admissions, 4 IIT admissions, and 4 NEET admissions, GTR EDUTECH is committed to delivering measurable results and academic excellence across tier-2 and tier-3 cities.
